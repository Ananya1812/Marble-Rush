INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Match Marbles: Swap adjacent marbles to create groups of three or more of the same color.
  2. Create Special Marbles: Matching four marbles generates a special one clearing a row or column; matching five or more forms a powerful marble with broader clearing abilities.
  3. Utilize Boosters: Earn boosters by making significant combinations; use them strategically to clear larger areas or target specific spots.
  4. Overcome Obstacles: Break ice, chains, or stones by matching adjacent marbles, clearing the path to progress.
  5. Manage Limits: Some levels have move or time constraints; achieve objectives within these limits to advance.
  6. Level Goals: Clear a set number of marbles, reach a score, or free trapped items to complete each level.
